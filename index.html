About Us

Welcome to Simply Rooted Microgreens!

Fresh. Nutrient-packed. Sustainable.

These are the three tenets that define our microgreen business. Founded in 2020 by Simply Rooted Farms, we started with a simple mission: to provide our community with the freshest, most nutritious greens while respecting our environment.

Why Microgreens?

Microgreens aren't just regular greens; they're super-greens! Harvested just after the first leaves have developed, microgreens are packed with more nutrients and flavor than their mature counterparts. They're a perfect addition to any dish, adding a punch of nutrition and a vibrant burst of color.

Our Process

Our microgreens are grown right here in Bridgewater, New Jersey. We use only organic seeds and a soilless medium, ensuring that what you get is 100% natural. Our controlled indoor environment allows us to grow year-round, so you always have access to fresh greens regardless of the season.

Sustainable Practices

We believe in a sustainable future. Our growing process uses 90% less water than traditional farming. We also utilize biodegradable packaging and deliver our products using eco-friendly methods.

Join Us in Our Green Revolution!

Whether you're a chef looking to spice up your dishes or a health enthusiast wanting the best for your body, we're here for you. For fresh, sustainable, and delicious microgreens, look no further. Dive into the world of superfoods with Simply Rooted Microgreens!
